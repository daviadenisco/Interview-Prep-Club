interview prep club

About

The Galvanize Interview Prep Club engages members from the Galvanize community, as well as students and graduates from the NYC campus in two meetups each week. Each meetup will begin with a member from the Galvanize community sharing information about their interview process and their expectations of candidates.

Attendees will learn more about: phone screens, tech screens, whiteboarding expectations and sample questions, sample take home assessments, what to expect in code reviews, onsite interviews, etc.
