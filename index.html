Galvanize Interview Prep Club

About

The Galvanize Interview Prep Club engages members, current students and graduates in two meetings each week. Each meeting will begin with a member from the Galvanize community sharing information about their interview process and what they expect from candidates. Attendees can expect to learn more about: phone screens, tech screens, whiteboarding expectations and sample questions, sample take home assessments, what to expect in code reviews, onsite interviews, etc.
